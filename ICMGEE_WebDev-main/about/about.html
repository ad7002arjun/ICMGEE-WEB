Scope of the Conference

Artificial Intelligence (AI), a groundbreaking technology, has emerged as the driving force behind the Industrial Revolution 5.0. For IR 5.0, with its human-centric orientation, AI was developed in combination with human intelligence (HI), leading to the new concept of Augmented Intelligence (AuI). AI and AuI algorithms are expected to bring significant benefits for enabling smart manufacturing in Industry 5.0. AI has become a powerful tool that can analyze vast amounts of data, uncover patterns, and make predictions, enabling informed decision-making across various sectors.

The enabling technologies related to Industry 5.0 include Cloud Computing, Blockchain, Analytics of Big Data, IoT and 6G Networks, Robotics, Nanotechnology, and Biotechnology. In fields such as finance, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, supply chain management, logistics, and utilities, AI is disrupting traditional processes and introducing innovative solutions that streamline operations, enhance productivity, and improve customer experiences. By harnessing the potential of AI while maintaining transparency, accountability, and an ethical framework, we can ensure that Industrial Revolution 5.0 is a force for good and delivers positive outcomes for all of humanity.
